About Climate Change Players

Climate Climate Change Players is a visualization which explores how the countries of the world contribute to world emissions. It's purpose is to let it's users make quick correlations between countries and the respective attribute those countries have, such as: different emissions, population, GDP, etc

You can use the selector to choose the data you wish to see mapped to the globe, simply drag your mouse over the years you want to see for that data. Once you've chosen your data, you can select countries that you're interested in and compare them below. Again you can select the data you want to compare here.

Climate Change Players lets you draw your own conclusions and son how different countries are effecting the world
